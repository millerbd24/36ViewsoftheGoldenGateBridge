Brad Miller

Brad Miller is a Bay Area native, currently living in Marin County, whose photography explores the intersection of landscapes, humanity, community, and wildlife that define California's identity. Drawing on a lifetime of immersion in the Bay Area's extraordinary diversity of life and geography.
